How It Works

Four steps. That's it.

Japanese Interval Walking is simple by design. IntervalWalker handles the timing so you can focus on the walk.

1

Choose a profile

Pick from Beginner, Standard, or Advanced — or create custom intervals from 1 to 10 minutes per phase with 1 to 15 sets.

2

Walk fast

When the orange ring starts, walk at a brisk pace — around 70% of your max effort. Voice cues and haptics tell you when to start.

3

Walk slow

When the cyan ring appears, ease back to a comfortable pace. Recover, catch your breath, and let your heart rate come down.

4

Repeat & review

Complete your sets, then review your stats — distance, heart rate per phase, and route. Build your streak over time.

The Science

15+ years of research.

Japanese Interval Walking was developed at Shinshu University and has been studied for over 15 years. Research shows that alternating 3 minutes of fast walking (~70% max effort) with 3 minutes of slow walking, repeated 5 times, produces greater improvements in cardiovascular fitness, muscle strength, and blood pressure than steady-pace walking of the same duration.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Overview

IntervalWalker is a local-only fitness app. All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. IntervalWalker has no user accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no server-side components.

Data Stored On Your Device

IntervalWalker stores the following data locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch:

  • Walk sessions: Date, duration, sets completed, interval timings, steps, distance, calories, and heart rate data. Stored via SwiftData on-device.
  • Settings preferences: Interval durations, set count, music and feedback toggles, selected playlists. Stored in UserDefaults on-device.
  • Widget data: Walking streak, weekly session count, and last walk date. Stored in shared App Group UserDefaults on-device.

Third-Party Services

IntervalWalker integrates with the following Apple frameworks. All data stays within the Apple ecosystem on your device:

  • HealthKit: With your permission, IntervalWalker reads heart rate data during walks and writes workout sessions (including GPS routes) to Apple Health. IntervalWalker does not share Health data with any third party.
  • Apple Music (MusicKit): With your permission, IntervalWalker accesses your Apple Music library to play music during walks. IntervalWalker does not access your listening history or share music data.
  • Core Location: With your permission, IntervalWalker records your GPS route during walks and saves it with your workout in Apple Health. Location data is not transmitted or stored outside of Apple Health.
  • WatchConnectivity: IntervalWalker communicates between your iPhone and Apple Watch to sync walk state and settings. This communication is device-to-device only.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. Period.

Data Deletion

All IntervalWalker data can be deleted by:

  • Deleting the app from your iPhone and/or Apple Watch
  • Deleting individual workout records from the Apple Health app

Children's Privacy

IntervalWalker does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
